# Sober Streak Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Sober Streak helps users track habit progress, journal entries, milestones, and optional community support.

## Data We Collect

- Account information: email address and display name for sign-in.
- Habit data: habit type, name, quit/start date, daily cost, and progress settings.
- Journal data: mood, notes, triggers, and dates entered by the user.
- Progress history: past streak periods, reflections, triggers, and estimated savings.
- Community content: anonymous posts and support reactions submitted by users.

## How Data Is Used

Data is used to provide streak tracking, statistics, journal history, account backup, and community features. Sober Streak does not sell personal data.

## Storage And Sync

The app stores data locally on the device using secure platform storage where available. When signed in, supported data syncs to Supabase so it can be restored on another device. Supabase authentication manages passwords and sessions.

## Community Privacy

Community posts appear anonymous by default. Users should not share phone numbers, addresses, medical information, or other sensitive personal details in community posts.

## Payments

The first store release is a free MVP. Future payment providers may process subscription or purchase information under their own privacy terms.

## Data Deletion

Users can clear local journal and progress history from the app's Data & Privacy screen. Account-level deletion requests can be sent to the developer contact listed in the store listing.

## Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact the developer through the support email listed on the App Store or Google Play listing.
